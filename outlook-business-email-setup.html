Connecting Your Nordible Business Email to Microsoft Outlook (Desktop & Mobile)

Relying on webmail interfaces can sometimes feel restrictive, especially if you manage multiple domains or require robust offline access. Fortunately, because Nordible Mail natively supports standard email and synchronization protocols, you can seamlessly link your mailboxes to Microsoft Outlook on both your desktop and your smartphone.

Connecting to Outlook unlocks a significant advantage: Exchange ActiveSync (EAS) support. Unlike standard IMAP, which only synchronizes your emails, ActiveSync allows Outlook to fully sync your emails, calendars, and contacts simultaneously without requiring third-party plugins.

Part 1: Microsoft Outlook for Desktop (Windows & Mac)

The desktop version of Outlook handles Nordible Mail excellently. For the smoothest setup experience, our servers include built-in autodiscover protocols that allow Outlook to automatically find your server settings using just your email address and password.

Step 1

Initialize Account Setup

  1. Launch Microsoft Outlook on your computer.
  2. If this is a fresh install, a setup prompt will appear automatically. Otherwise, click File in the top-left corner and select Add Account.
Step 2

Automatic Discovery (Recommended)

  1. Enter your complete business email address (e.g., you@yourbusiness.com).
  2. Click Connect. Outlook will attempt to scan your domain's DNS records for Nordible's autodiscover configuration.
  3. If successful, a prompt will appear requesting your account credentials. Enter your email password and click OK.
Step 3

Manual Fallback (If Autodiscover is Disabled)

If your domain's DNS is not yet optimized for automatic discovery, you can quickly input the connection coordinates manually:

  1. Select Advanced Options under the email input field and check "Let me set up my account manually".
  2. Choose IMAP from the account type selection grid.
  3. Populate the server details as follows:
Incoming Mail Server: mail.nordible.co
Port: 993 | Encryption: SSL/TLS
Outgoing Mail Server (SMTP): mail.nordible.co
Port: 465 | Encryption: SSL/TLS

Click Next, input your email password, and click Connect.

Part 2: Microsoft Outlook for Mobile (iOS & Android)

The mobile Outlook application is highly optimized for performance and handles push notifications for Nordible mailboxes effortlessly through Exchange ActiveSync.

Step 1

Add a New Account

  1. Open the Outlook app on your mobile device.
  2. Tap the Home icon or your profile avatar in the top-left corner, then tap the Gear icon (Settings) at the bottom.
  3. Select Add Mail Account, then tap Add an Email Account.
Step 2

Let Outlook Detect the Server

  1. Type your full business email address and tap Continue.
  2. The app will attempt to automatically detect the architecture. If it prompts you to choose an account provider, choose Exchange (or Advanced Setup > Exchange).
Step 3

Configure ActiveSync

To ensure your mail, calendar, and contacts all sync to your phone in real time, enter your parameters using the ActiveSync layout:

Email Address: you@yourbusiness.com
Password: Your email password
Server: mail.nordible.co
Domain\Username: (Enter your full email address)
Description: Work Email

Tap the Checkmark or Sign In button. Your data will begin downloading immediately.

Optimization Pro-Tip: Setting Up DNS Autodiscover

To make this process completely painless for your team, you should map Autodiscover records in your domain's DNS zone file. This allows Outlook to configure itself instantly with just an email and password:

Type Host/Name Value/Target
CNAME autodiscover mail.nordible.co.
SRV _autodiscover._tcp 0 0 443 mail.nordible.co.

Once these records propagate, setup becomes a one-click process.
