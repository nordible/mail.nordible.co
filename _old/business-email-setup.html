Step 1

Point Your Domain to Nordible

To receive emails, you need to tell your domain provider (like GoDaddy or Namecheap) where to send them. Log in to your registrar and update your DNS Settings with this MX record:

Type Host Points to Priority
MX @ mail.nordible.co 10

Too technical? Don't worry! Share your registrar login details securely via our WhatsApp chat, and our engineers will do this for you for free.

Step 2

Step 3

Ensure You Reach the Inbox

To make sure your invoices and pitches don't end up in your client's spam folder, add these two security records to your DNS settings:

SPF Record (TXT)

v=spf1 include:mail.nordible.co ~all

DKIM Record (TXT)

(Unique key provided in your welcome email)

These records prove to the world that you are the legitimate owner of your domain.
